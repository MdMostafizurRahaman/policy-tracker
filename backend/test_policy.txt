Test AI Policy Document

This is a sample AI transparency policy for testing purposes.

Transparency Requirements:
- All AI systems must provide clear explanations to users
- Decision-making processes must be documented and accessible
- Public consultations were conducted during policy development

Explainability Standards:
- AI outputs must include reasoning behind decisions
- Users have the right to understand how AI affects them
- Technical documentation must be available for review

Accountability Framework:
- Clear oversight mechanisms are established
- Responsible parties are identified for each AI system
- Regular audits and assessments are conducted
- Appeal processes are available for affected individuals

This policy ensures responsible AI development and deployment.
